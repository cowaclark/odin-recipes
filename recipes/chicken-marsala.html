Chicken Marsala

This dish contains chicken breast covered with a marsala wine marinade and topped with mushrooms and prosciutto.

Ingredients

  • 1.5lbs Chicken Breast
  • All-purpose flour for dredging
  • 8oz halved mushrooms
  • 4oz thinly sliced prosciutto
  • 1/2cup Marsala wine
  • 1/2cup chicken stock
  • 2tbspn butter

Steps

  1. First, slice the chicken breasts in half, then pound until they are roughly 1/4" thick. Place the chicken in flour that has been seasoned with a fair amount of salt and pepper, dredging each side before placing on stove.
  2. Oil pan, then place chicken on pan and fry for about 5 minutes each side until golden brown and cooked through. Remove chicken to side plate/platter.
  3. Lower heat to medium and add prosciutto. Saute for roughly 1 minute. Add mushrooms until nicely browned for about 5 minutes. Season well. Pour marsala in the pan to allow it to boil down before adding the chicken stock. Simmer gently for 1 minute. Then, Stir in butter and add chicken breats. Simmer once more for 1 minute to heat the chicken all the way through. Season to preferred liking and prepare on a plate.
