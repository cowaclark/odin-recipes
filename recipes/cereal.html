Cereal

This easy yet satisfying dish is every young man's go to before the fully have to grow up into an adult.

Ingredients

  • 1-2cups of preferred cereal
  • Bowl
  • 1/2-2cups of preferred milk

Steps

  1. First, open your box of cereal. Then pour contents into bowl.
  2. Then, add milk to your desired amount of dilution.
  3. Place spoon in bowl, gather cereal, and move the delightful crunchy creations towards your mouth and enjoy!
